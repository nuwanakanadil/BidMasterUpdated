Welcome to our BidMaster FAQ page!

What is BidMaster ?

BidMaster is an online auction platform where you can bid on items in real time.

How to participate ?

You can participate by creating an account and joining active auctions.

Is there a fee ?

Some auctions may have fees, which are clearly stated in their terms.

How do I receive my won items ?

Items can either be shipped or picked up based on the auction's rules.
